About Us

Even if we think that you know us, (yes, we love to travel and already performed our show to 24 different places in 11 countries!) let’s talk a few about the magic we want to throw at your eyes :)

Since 2014, our philosophy is to entertain people, so, but our first goal is to spread joy around the globe. Yes, we think we can change the world !

Because "a day without laughter is a day wasted" as Charlie Chaplin said, and so we want to put a smile on your face for the next 2 hours when you you enter the circus tent.

Here at Wild Circus, we also are a family. A great family of around 2,000 performers and ex-performers ! You can be a part of this family, and apply to learn with our enthusiast team in several countries in Europe.

Last, don’t forget when the enter the tent: choose and put your most beautiful socks ! Yes, it’s scientifically proven, without shoes the joy spreads easier ;)
